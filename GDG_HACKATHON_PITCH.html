❌ The Problem

Why Students Are Falling Behind

In Pakistan and across the developing world, millions of students lack access to quality, personalized education. Traditional classrooms cannot adapt to every individual's pace, learning style, or emotional state.

📚

One-size-fits-all teaching ignores individual learning styles and pace

💸

Quality tutoring is expensive and inaccessible for most Pakistani families

🧠

No tools exist to understand a student's psychological learning state

🌐

Language barrier — all major AI education tools work in English only

📉

Students don't know their weak areas or how to systematically improve

😟

No system to track student motivation, emotional wellness or consistency

✅ Our Solution

Pak AI Tutor — One Platform, Every Student's Need

A single comprehensive platform combining intelligent tutoring, personalized assessments, psychological profiling, bilingual support, and gamification — making world-class education available to every student, free of charge.

🤖

AI Personal Tutor

24/7 AI tutor that answers any academic question, draws geometric shapes and solves problems step-by-step in both English and authentic Pakistani Urdu.

🧬

PAT Psychological Assessment

Our flagship feature — a 5-step Personal Assessment Test that analyzes learning style, motivation, emotional wellness and generates a personalized AI learning profile.

🧠

Mastery-Based Learning

📸

AI Homework Solver

🇵🇰 Bilingual Support

Built for Pakistani Students — Full Urdu Support

Most AI education tools worldwide are English-only. Pak AI Tutor is designed from the ground up for Pakistani students who think, read, and learn in Urdu.

Authentic Pakistani Urdu

AI responds in proper Pakistani Urdu — not machine-translated Hindi. We explicitly train the AI to use Pakistani vocabulary (خوش آمدید not سواگت, براہ کرم not کرپیا).

Jameel Noori Nastaleeq Font

Beautiful right-to-left Urdu display using the Jameel Noori Nastaleeq font — the authentic calligraphy style Pakistani students are familiar with.
